# Moviecure

App where you review movies and get recommended critics with similar taste. I picked this because I love movies and finding good movies I haven't seen before. I thought it was an interesting idea to recommend critics instead of movies themselves.

## Description

Moviecure lets you log and rate movies you've watched, then matches you withcritics who share your taste — so you can discover new films and read reviews from someone who thinks like you.
